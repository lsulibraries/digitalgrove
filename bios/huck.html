Henry N. Huck

1894 - 1917

Henry Nicholson Huck was born on December 17, 1894 in Independence, Louisiana to Mr. and Mrs. H.A. Huck. He graduated from Independence High School on March 14, 1913 and entered LSU on September 16, 1916 where he studied chemical engineering. On September 17, 1917, Huck became a private at Camp Pike, Arkansas and was soon promoted to sergeant and transferred to Camp Beauregard, Louisiana. Huck died at Camp Beauregard on December 7, 1917. He is buried in Amite, Louisiana.
